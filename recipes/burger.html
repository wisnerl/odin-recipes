Smash Burger Recipe

Description:

Who doesn't love a good burger? This is a great one

Ingredients:

  • Ground Chuck
  • Buns
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Yellow Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo

Steps:

  1. Combine the ketchup, mustard, and mayo in a bowl
  2. Slice the onions, lettuce and Tomato
  3. Roll the burgers into 4 equal sized balls and place in a hot pan
  4. Smash the burgers down with a spatula and flip when one side is cooked
  5. Toast your buns and place the pattys on them
  6. Top with your sauce and vegetables and enjoy!
