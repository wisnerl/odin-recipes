Chopped Salad Recipe

Description:

Time for a healthy recipe after all of the other two

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Carrot
  • Feta Cheese
  • Dressing of your choice

Steps:

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Dump the vegetables in a bowl
  3. Add the cheese on top
  4. Add your preferred dressing over the top
  5. Enjoy!
