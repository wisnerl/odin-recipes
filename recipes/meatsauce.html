Pasta and Meat Sauce Recipe

Description:

This is a classic dish in my family. The sauce can be made completely from scratch to be authentic, but it is still very good with a store bought sauce

Ingredients:

  • Linguine
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Meat

Steps:

  1. Boil water and when ready, add the Linguine to the the pot
  2. Cook up the meat in a large sauce pan (can optionally add garlic and onions with the meat)
  3. When everything is cooked, add the sauce to the pan and let simmer until the pasta is ready
  4. When the pasta is ready, strain the water out and then add the noodles to the pan
  5. Stir to incorporate the noodles and the sauce
  6. Enjoy!
